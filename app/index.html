Air-Fried Vegetable Omelette


PREP 10 MIN


COOK 15 MIN


SERVES 3

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup diced bell peppers (any color)
  • 1/4 cup diced tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons diced onions
  • 2 tablespoons chopped spinach
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon grated low-fat cheese (optional)

Directions

  1. Preheat your air fryer to 350°F (180°C).
  2. In a bowl, whisk the eggs until well beaten.
  3. Add diced bell peppers, tomatoes, onions, and chopped spinach to the beaten eggs. Mix well.
  4. Add olive oil to the mixture and season with salt and pepper. Stir until all ingredients are evenly combined.
  5. Lightly grease the air fryer basket with cooking spray or a small amount of olive oil.
  6. Pour the egg mixture into the air fryer basket.
  7. If desired, sprinkle grated low-fat cheese on top of the omelette mixture.
  8. Place the basket in the preheated air fryer and cook for 8 minutes or until the edges are golden brown and the omelette is cooked through.
  9. Carefully remove the air-fried omelette from the basket.
  10. Allow it to cool for a minute before slicing and serving.

Nutritional Information:  Calories: 250, Sugar: 2g, Protein: 15g, Carbohydrates: 6g, Fat: 18g, Fiber: 2g, Cholesterol: 370mg, Sodium: 350mg, Potassium: 300mg.

Cinnamon Apple Air-Fryer Pancakes


PREP 10 MIN


COOK 8 MIN


SERVES 2

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup coconut flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon melted coconut oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 medium apple, peeled, cored, and finely chopped

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together almond flour, coconut flour, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs and then add almond milk, melted coconut oil, and vanilla extract. Mix well.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Fold in the chopped apple.
  4. Preheat your air fryer to 350°F (180°C).
  5. Lightly grease the air fryer basket with coconut oil or use parchment paper to prevent sticking.
  6. Drop spoonfuls of the batter onto the air fryer basket, leaving space between each pancake.
  7. Air fry for 4 minutes, then flip the pancakes and air fry for an additional 4 minutes or until they are golden brown and cooked through.
  8. Remove the pancakes from the air fryer and let them cool for a minute before serving.

Nutritional Information (per serving):  Calories: 180, Sugar: 3g, Protein: 7g, Carbohydrates: 10g, Fat: 14g, Fiber: 4g, Cholesterol: 62mg, Sodium: 210mg, Potassium: 180mg

Spinach and Feta Egg Bites


PREP 10 MIN


COOK 10 MIN


SERVES 2

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tablespoons red bell pepper, finely diced
  • 1 tablespoon green onions, finely chopped
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Cooking spray

Directions

  1. Preheat your air fryer to 350°F (180°C).
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs until well beaten.
  3. Add the chopped spinach, crumbled feta cheese, diced red bell pepper, green onions, black pepper, and garlic powder to the beaten eggs. Mix well until all ingredients are evenly combined.
  4. Lightly coat the wells of an air fryer egg bite mold with cooking spray to prevent sticking.
  5. Lay out the low-carb whole wheat tortillas and divide the scrambled eggs evenly between them.
  6. Pour the egg mixture evenly into the mold, filling each well about three-quarters full.
  7. Place the filled mold in the preheated air fryer basket.
  8. Cook the egg bites at 350°F (180°C) for 12 minutes or until the eggs are set and the tops are lightly golden.
  9. Carefully remove the mold from the air fryer and let it cool for a few minutes before gently popping out the egg bites.
  10. Serve the Spinach and Feta Egg Bites warm. Optionally, garnish with additional chopped green onions or a sprinkle of black pepper.

Nutritional Information: Calories: 220, Sugar: 1g, Protein: 16g, Carbohydrates: 3g, Fat: 16g, Fiber: 1g, Cholesterol: 390mg, Sodium: 370mg, Potassium: 250mg

Low-Carb Air-Fried Breakfast Burritos


PREP 10 MIN


COOK 10 MIN


TOTAL 20 MIN


SERVES 2

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup diced bell peppers (any color)
  • 1/4 cup diced onions
  • 1/4 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 low-carb whole wheat tortillas
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: salsa, avocado slices, cilantro

Directions

  1. Preheat your air fryer to 350°F (180°C).
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and almond flour until well combined. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Sauté diced bell peppers, onions, and tomatoes until they are tender, about 3-5 minutes.
  4. Pour the egg mixture over the sautéed vegetables and scramble until the eggs are fully cooked.
  5. Lay out the low-carb whole wheat tortillas and divide the scrambled eggs evenly between them.
  6. Sprinkle shredded cheddar cheese over the eggs on each tortilla.
  7. Fold the sides of the tortillas inward and then roll them up tightly to form burritos.
  8. Place the burritos in the preheated air fryer basket, seam side down. Air fry for 5-7 minutes or until the tortillas are golden and crispy.
  9. Remove the burritos from the air fryer and let them cool for a minute before slicing in half.
  10. Serve with optional toppings such as salsa, avocado slices, and cilantro.

Nutritional Information (per serving): Calories: 280, Sugar: 3g, Protein: 16g, Carbohydrates: 20g, Fat: 15g, Fiber: 5g, Cholesterol: 185mg, Sodium: 350mg, Potassium: 450mg

Zucchini and Cheese Fritters


PREP 15 MIN


COOK 15 MIN


SERVES 2

Ingredients

  • ● 1 medium zucchini, grated
  • 1/2 cup shredded low-fat cheddar cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons almond flour
  • ●1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 whole wheat low-carb tortillas
  • Cooking spray

Directions

  1. Preheat your air fryer to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the grated zucchini, cheddar cheese, eggs, almond flour, baking powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all ingredients are evenly combined.
  3. Lightly spray the air fryer basket with cooking spray to prevent sticking.
  4. Form the zucchini mixture into small fritters and place them in the air fryer basket, leaving space between each fritter.
  5. Air fry the fritters for 10-12 minutes or until they are golden brown and cooked through. Flip them halfway through the cooking time for even browning.
  6. While the fritters are cooking, warm the whole wheat low-carb tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave according to package instructions.
  7. Once the fritters are done, assemble the breakfast burritos by placing two fritters in the center of each tortilla.
  8. Fold the sides of the tortilla over the fritters and then fold the bottom and top to enclose the filling, creating a burrito.
  9. Serve the breakfast burritos warm and enjoy your delicious and diabetes-friendly meal!

Nutritional Information: Calories: 280, Sugar: 3g, Protein: 16g, Carbohydrates: 20g, Fat: 15g, Fiber: 5g, Cholesterol: 185mg, Sodium: 350mg, Potassium: 450mg
